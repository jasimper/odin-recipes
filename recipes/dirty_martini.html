Dirty Martini

Description

I like a completely dry martini so I like to dirty it up with brine. Olive is my least favorite. Pickle, onion, and banana peppers are more fun imho.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 oz Gin or Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Dry Vermouth or a splash of water
  • 0.5 oz Brine of your choice
  • Ice for mixing

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker
  2. Shake for 15 seconds
  3. Pour into a martini glass
  4. Enjoy
