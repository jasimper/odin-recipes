Gin and Jam

Description

This is a great summer sipper and can be any flavor your like. I'm a fan of apple jelly for this, but you do you.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 1 Tbsp Jam or Jelly
  • 0.5 oz Lemon Juice
  • 0.5 oz Simple Syrup
  • Ice for mixing

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker
  2. Shake for 30 seconds
  3. Pour through a strainer into a glass
  4. Enjoy
