Sweet Word

Description

This is my take on the Last Word. I do love the original, but I sometimes want something a little sweeter.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Gin
  • 0.75 oz Genepy
  • 0.5 oz Luxardo
  • 0.75 oz Lime Juice
  • 0.75 oz Simple Syrup
  • Ice for mixing

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker
  2. Shake for 15 seconds
  3. Pour into a martini glass
  4. Enjoy
